Call First Strategy

Agents tagged Call first control 75+ active listings. They are the highest-ROI targets because one relationship unlocks recurring roof cert work.

📞 Why these agents first? Click to expand
  • Volume: Windermere, More Realty, and Premiere control 75 listings today. That's 75 potential inspections before summer.
  • FHA/VA dependency: First-time buyers use FHA/VA loans. Those loans require roof certification. Agents who sell $400k-$600k homes hit this constantly.
  • Speed wins: Agents are burned by roofers who ghost them or take 2 weeks to show up. If you answer on the first ring and inspect within 48 hours, you become their default.
  • Recurring revenue: A good agent lists 8-15 homes per year. Each one needs a pre-listing inspection or buyer cert. One agent = $10k-$25k/year.
Script 1 — Cold Call

"Hi [Agent Name], I'm [Name] with [Company]. I specialize in fast roof certifications for FHA and VA buyers — 48-hour turnaround, flat fee. I noticed you have a listing on [Address]. Do any of your current listings need a roof cert, or would a free pre-listing inspection help you price it right?"

Script 2 — Follow-up Text (after first call)

"Hi [Agent Name], it's [Name] from [Company]. Following up on the roof cert conversation. I'm attaching a one-page rate sheet + 3 recent before/afters from [Neighborhood]. Happy to do a free pre-listing inspection on your toughest listing to prove the quality. Let me know!"

Script 3 — Door knock (sold homes)

"Hi, I'm [Name] with [Company]. We're doing roof inspections in [Neighborhood] this week. Most home inspectors only glance at the roof from the driveway — we check attic ventilation, flashing, and surface condition. It's $99 for new homeowners, takes 30 minutes, and you get a written report for your insurance. Can I schedule you for Thursday or Friday?"
